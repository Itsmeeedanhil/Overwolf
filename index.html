ABOUT THE OVERWOLF
ESPORT TEAMS

The Overwolf esports team is a highly skilled and experienced group of professional gamers who compete in various esports tournaments and events.

They specialize in a variety of popular games and are known for their strategic thinking, teamwork, and communication skills. They are committed to the gaming community and use gaming as a force for good.

As part of the Overwolf software company, they embody professionalism, sportsmanship, and dedication to the gaming community.
